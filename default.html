African Women's Agri-Tech Initiative Project

Mission:

Empowering marginalized women in Uganda, including refugee women, through agriculture technology, education, and economic opportunities, to improve their livelihoods, food security, and overall well-being.

Project Overview:

This project addresses the intersectional challenges faced by marginalized women in Uganda, including limited access to land, credit, and markets, as well as the impacts of climate change and gender-based violence. By providing training, technology, and economic opportunities, we will empower 500 women to become successful agricultural entrepreneurs, improving their incomes, food security, and overall well-being.
